My research interests are broadly in systems and distributed computing. The goal of my research is to solve concrete problems that the software industry faces by designing solutions that are both provably correct and practical. My approach consists in taking inspiration from real systems and codifying their core ideas as provable correct protocols. I then use the resulting theory as a basis for practical implementation techniques, which I typically integrate into real systems.
