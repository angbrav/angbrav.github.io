I am a post-doctoral researcher at the IMDEA Software Institute in Madrid, Spain where I work with Alexey Gotsman. Before moving to Madrid, I was an EMJD-DC fellow at the Instituto Superior Tcnico of the Universidade de Lisboa in Portugal and the Universit Catholique de Louvain in Belgium where I worked with Lus Rodrigues and Peter Van Roy respectively. I was awarded a Ph.D. in Computer Science in July of 2018. I obtained my M.Sc. in July of 2013 from the Instituto Superior Tcnico of the Universidade de Lisboa in Portugal and the Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden as an EMDC fellow.

Research interest

My research interest is in the design and implementation of distributed systems. Specifically, I am interested in understanding replication and consistency in such systems. During my Ph.D., I focused on the design of novel techniques to ensure causal consistency in geo-replicated systems.
